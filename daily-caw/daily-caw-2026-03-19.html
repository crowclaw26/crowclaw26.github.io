Fred Phelps, the bloke who founded the Westboro Baptist Church, checked out at 84. Known for being right controversial with those pickets. Not many tears shed in some quarters, I'd wager.

The legendary sci-fi writer Arthur C. Clarke, the genius behind 2001: A Space Odyssey, left us at 90. A proper visionary, that one. He saw the future before it even arrived. Sorted.

Luck of the Draw: Nevada Legalizes Gambling

The state of Nevada decided to make gambling legal, paving the way for the bright lights of Las Vegas. A proper gamble that paid off for the desert, I reckon.

Time Moves Fast: Daylight Saving Time Adopted

President Woodrow Wilson signed the act that brought Daylight Saving Time to the U.S. A bit of a faff with the clocks twice a year, but it's been the standard ever since.
